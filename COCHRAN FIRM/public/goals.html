GOALS OF THE FIRM

Cochran Law Firm, established by the renowned attorney Johnnie L. Cochran Jr., has a mission deeply rooted in a commitment to justice and advocacy for the underserved and marginalized. The firm's goals reflect its dedication to providing high-quality legal representation and making a positive impact on the legal system and society. Here are the primary goals of the Cochran Law Firm:

Cochran Law Firm strives to uphold justice by ensuring that every client receives fair and just representation, regardless of their background or socio-economic status. The firm is dedicated to fighting for the rights of individuals who may not have the resources to stand up against powerful entities on their own.

A significant part of the firm's legacy and ongoing mission is to protect and advance civil rights. This includes taking on cases involving police misconduct, racial discrimination, and other violations of constitutional rights, continuing the work Johnnie Cochran was famously known for.

Beyond legal representation, the firm is committed to empowering communities through education and outreach. This includes hosting seminars, workshops, and providing resources to help individuals understand their legal rights and how to protect them.

Maintaining the highest standards of ethical practice and integrity is a core goal of the Cochran Law Firm. The firm is dedicated to practicing law with honesty, transparency, and a strong moral compass, ensuring that all actions taken are in the best interest of the clients and the justice system.

The firm seeks to innovate within the legal field by employing creative and effective legal strategies. This involves staying abreast of new legal precedents, technological advancements, and novel approaches to case management and trial advocacy.

With multiple offices across the United States, the Cochran Law Firm aims to provide accessible legal services to a broad audience while maintaining a focus on local community needs. This dual approach ensures that clients receive personalized attention and that the firm can address regional legal issues effectively.

Honoring the legacy of its founder, the firm is committed to building and sustaining a legacy of legal excellence. This involves not only winning cases but also contributing to significant legal precedents and societal change.

The Cochran Law Firm aims to provide top-tier legal services to its clients. This goal is achieved by employing a team of highly skilled and experienced attorneys who are experts in various fields of law, including personal injury, criminal defense, civil litigation, and wrongful death cases.
