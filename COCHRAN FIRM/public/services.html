Services Provided by The Cochran Law Firm

Personal Injury

The Cochran Law Firm specializes in personal injury cases, providing legal assistance to those who have suffered harm due to the negligence or wrongdoing of others. This includes:

  • Car Accidents: Representing victims of car, truck, motorcycle, and pedestrian accidents.
  • Medical Malpractice: Handling cases involving surgical errors, misdiagnoses, and other forms of medical negligence.
  • Slip and Fall Incidents: Advocating for individuals injured on unsafe properties.
  • Wrongful Death Claims: Assisting families who have lost loved ones due to another party's negligence.

Civil Rights

Committed to justice and equality, The Cochran Law Firm represents clients in civil rights cases involving:

  • Police Misconduct: Challenging cases of excessive force, false arrest, and other forms of police misconduct.
  • Racial Discrimination: Fighting against discriminatory practices in employment, housing, and public accommodations.
  • Workplace Harassment: Addressing sexual harassment, hostile work environments, and other unlawful workplace behaviors.
  • Violations of Constitutional Rights: Defending clients whose constitutional rights have been violated.

Criminal Defense

The Cochran Law Firm offers robust criminal defense services for individuals facing charges ranging from misdemeanors to serious felonies. This includes:

  • Drug Offenses: Defending clients against charges of possession, distribution, and trafficking of controlled substances.
  • DUI: Providing legal representation for individuals charged with driving under the influence.
  • White-Collar Crimes: Handling cases of fraud, embezzlement, insider trading, and other financial crimes.
  • Assault and Violent Crimes: Representing clients accused of assault, battery, domestic violence, and other violent offenses.

Product Liability

The Cochran Law Firm assists clients who have been harmed by defective products. Their experienced attorneys handle cases involving:

  • Defective Medical Devices: Representing individuals injured by faulty medical implants, surgical instruments, and other medical devices.
  • Dangerous Pharmaceuticals: Taking on cases where clients have suffered adverse effects from unsafe medications.
  • Consumer Products: Pursuing claims for injuries caused by defective household products, electronics, and other consumer goods.
  • Automotive Defects: Addressing injuries resulting from defective car parts, such as airbags, seat belts, and brakes.
